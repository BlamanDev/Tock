- You have to play an ACE, a KING or a JOKER to make a Pawn enter the Board

- If you can't play a card, you have to discard one
- If your Pawn arrive on another Pawn cell, this one will be eaten
- A Pawn on his Entry cannot be passed, moved or eaten
- A Pawn on House Cells cannot be eaten

- The Winner is the player who succeed to bring his all four pawn to their house cells
